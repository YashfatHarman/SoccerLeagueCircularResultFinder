La Liga 1959-60 season was the 29th since its establishment. The season started on September 13, 1959 and finished on April 17, 1960. It consisted of the following clubs:

  • Atlético Bilbao
  • Atlético Madrid
  • CF Barcelona
  • Elche CF
  • RCD Espanyol
  • Granada CF
  • UD Las Palmas
  • CA Osasuna
  
  • Real Betis
  • Real Madrid C.F.
  • Real Oviedo
  • Real Sociedad
  • Real Valladolid
  • Real Zaragoza
  • Sevilla FC
  • Valencia CF
