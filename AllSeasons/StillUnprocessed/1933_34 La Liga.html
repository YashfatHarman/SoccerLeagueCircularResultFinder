La Liga 1933-34 season started on November 5, 1933 and finished on March 4, 1934.

The season was composed of the following clubs:

  • Arenas Club de Getxo
  • Athletic Bilbao
  • FC Barcelona
  • Betis Balompié
  • Donostia CF
  
  • CE Espanyol
  • Madrid CF
  • Oviedo CF
  • Racing de Santander
  • Valencia CF
