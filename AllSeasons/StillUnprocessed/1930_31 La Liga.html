The 1930-31 La Liga season started December 7, 1930 and finished April 5, 1931. It was composed of the following clubs:

  • Arenas Club de Getxo
  • Athletic Bilbao
  • FC Barcelona
  • Deportivo Alavés
  • RCD Español
  
  • CE Europa
  • Racing de Santander
  • Real Madrid
  • Real Sociedad
  • Real Unión
