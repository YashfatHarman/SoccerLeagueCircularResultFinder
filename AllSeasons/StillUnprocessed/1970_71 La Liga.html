La Liga 1970-71 season was the 40th since its establishment. The season started on September 12, 1970 and finished on April 18, 1971. It consisted of the following clubs:

  • Atlético Bilbao
  • Atlético Madrid
  • CF Barcelona
  • Celta de Vigo
  • Elche CF
  • RCD Español
  • Granada CF
  • UD Las Palmas
  
  • CD Málaga
  • Real Gijón
  • Real Madrid
  • Real Sociedad
  • CD Sabadell
  • Sevilla CF
  • Valencia CF
  • Zaragoza
