La Liga 1995–96 season, the 65th since its establishment, started on September 9, 1995 and finished on May 26, 1996. Real Madrid's impressive start was cut short by a mass injuries to a key players, and they wound up having an unimpressive season. It was composed of the following clubs:

Later, Sevilla FC and Celta de Vigo were also readmitted and the league was expanded to 22 teams for two seasons.
