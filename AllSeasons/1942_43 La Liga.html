La Liga 1942-43 season was the 12th since its establishment. It involved the following clubs:

  • Athletic Bilbao
  • Athletic Aviación de Madrid
  • FC Barcelona
  • CD Castellón
  • Celta de Vigo
  • Deportivo La Coruña
  • RCD Espanyol
  
  • Granada CF
  • Real Betis
  • Real Madrid C.F.
  • Real Oviedo
  • Real Zaragoza
  • Sevilla FC
  • Valencia CF
